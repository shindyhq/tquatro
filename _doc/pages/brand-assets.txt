# BRAND VOICE QUICK REFERENCE

*For anyone writing T-QUATRO copy — internal or external.*

---

## The Four T's — Our Brand Pillars

These four qualities are the foundation of every piece of T-QUATRO communication. Every page, post, email, and proposal should reflect at least one of them — ideally all four.

| Pillar | What It Means in Copy |
|---|---|
| **Transparency** | Honest language, no hedging, no overselling. Say what you mean. |
| **Transformation** | Lead with outcomes and change — not features and services. |
| **Tenacity** | Emphasize follow-through, execution, and staying power. |
| **Tailored** | Speak to the specific reader's situation, not a generic audience. |

---

## We Sound Like:
- Confident, not arrogant
- Direct, not blunt
- Knowledgeable, not academic
- Warm, not salesy
- Specific, not vague

## We Don't Say:
- "Synergistic solutions"
- "Paradigm-shifting"
- "Best-in-class"
- "Comprehensive suite of services"
- "Holistic approach"
- "We're passionate about..."

## We Do Say:
- "We stay in the room."
- "Outcomes, not activity."
- "Built for what commerce demands."
- "Strategy without execution is just a document."
- "We'll tell you honestly if we're the right fit."
- "Transparent. Transformative. Tenacious. Tailored."

## Tone Checklist:
- [ ] Is every sentence earning its place?
- [ ] Does this sound like something a real person would say?
- [ ] Are we leading with what the client gets — not what we do?
- [ ] Have we avoided hyperbole or unsupported claims?
- [ ] Would a skeptical business owner find this credible?
- [ ] Does this copy reflect at least one of the Four T's?
